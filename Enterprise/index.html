Is it for You?

In 2nd or 3rd year of an undergrad course? Enterprise is the perfect launchpad for you, if...

01

You want to study abroad

Start working towards winning a scholarship or a grant. That requires strategy, research, and time. At Enterprise, you will acquire the guidance and the resume bullet points required to winning a scholarship.

02

You want experience

We are advocates of gaining rich work experience. Identify and research the best fit organisation, understand your strengths, and craft your selling pitch. Acquire the necessary experience, skills and connections.

03

You want to do business

Nobody ever learns how to do business only through textbooks. Get out there and start meeting potential clients, facing rejections, raising funds, negotiating contracts, and inventing solutions, and all that. Ready?

Everything you need to know

Basically, how this program is going to rock your world.

Your Second Best 9 months

Enterprise is a 9-month long fellowship program for students in 2nd & 3rd year of college. It will be your 2nd best 9 months so far (the first was obviously in your mother’s womb!)

Build Your Brand

Planning to study abroad? Looking for scholarship? Want to get that job at one of the most innovative companies? Thinking of incubating your startup idea? Looking for funds? Everything depends on your personal brand - your story of what you have done, what you can do and what you want to do. At Enterprise, you will craft your brand along with the guidance from industry experts and mentors.

Start Earning

The Enterprise will give you opportunities to take up your first professional projects; you can take this opportunity & make it big.

Chill With Champions

The best learning happens outside the classroom, our informal dialogues and conversations. Every fortnight you will meet mentors from various industries to spend an informal evening and talk about everything that matters.

Learn By Doing

We have no books, teachers or lectures at Enterprise, yet you will learn faster than ever. Everything you learn will be through actual challenges and projects.

Coolest Office in Town

At Enterprise you will get to be in a young, funky & happening space that will be yours. There you will have access to latest tools & facilities which will enable you to DO.

2 Years of Career Support

The journey does not end after 9 months. The enterprise mentors will continue to be partners in your growth. Your success is our success, it’s as simple as that. You can leverage the networks, the guidance and even the coaching platform to help you fast track on your career journey.

Bootcamp

You will get to be the part of 4-day powerpack adventure field boot camp to kickstart your enterprise journey.
